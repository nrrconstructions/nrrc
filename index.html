Location

Delightful neighborhood of Osman Nagar

Haimi Nirvana stands as a sanctuary for residential living, offering a superb quality of life. Peaceful and meticulously planned, Osman Nagar provides seamless access to Hyderabad's dynamic centers such as Wipro Junction and Gachibowli via well-connected wide roads. Additionally, the Outer Ring Road (ORR) ensures a straightforward commute to the airport. If you are looking for an esteemed location that positions you amidst luxury, with proximity to premier shopping centers, fine dining venues, renowned schools, and multinational corporate establishments.
